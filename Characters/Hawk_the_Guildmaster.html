Sir Alderman Hawk the Guildmaster

Created Sunday 25 September 2022

Hawk is descended from a long line of glassmakers in Crystalthorp. He and his five younger brothers had to take over their father's business after he died of the coughing-sickness the winter of 1349 (21st year of King Ballendor's reign).

The business throve under Hawk's lead, but two of his brothers left, driven out by Hawk's domineering nature. He often nagged his brothers around and bade them do the most trivial of tasks for him. To the present day, he knows nothing of their whereabouts or wellbeing.

The business did not do as well as Hawk would have wished, and, being a very abitious and niggardly man, he turned to scheming rather than true effort to make his way upward amongst the glassblowers.

During this time, Hawk had done many things he would come to regret deeply in his old age, but his plans worked. Having driven out of business several of his betters, his shop was receiving more orders than it had ever before. By the time of Godswar he was amongst the highest ranks in the guild, being responsible for quality control on the vastest and most prestigious orders.

During wartime, Hawk distinguished himself as a great leader and protector. During the first days of Orks' invasion, he personally raised a millitia and would continue to cooperate with Lord Fyllior himself. During his Lords absence, he assumed some of his duties and proved himself invaluable.

After the war, in 1368, he was knighted by King Drachenwill and elected guildmaster.

At present, he continues to oversee the townlife as one of the town's aldermen. Having achieved success, his past deeds of dishonesty have begun to haunt him and as a penance, he has turned into a philanthropist, personally funding much of public merrymaking and donating large sums of coin to hospitals. He's married to a woman named Mildred, who suffers from delusions and schizophrenic bouts.
