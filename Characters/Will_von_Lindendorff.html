Will von Lindendorff

Created Monday 21 November 2022

Will is the son of a wealthy farmer from Dragonspearshire, the village of Lindendorff. During his formative years, he persuaded his father to permit him travel with his uncle up north to present-day Ashenwyrm where he would meet his bride-to-be Osthrith. After meeting her for only one time he decided he would have to take her for his wife. She happily agreed and the two of them returned to Lindendorff together mere months after meeting for the first time.

He had lived with his family in Loridia, in Lindendorr near Dragonspear Castle,, but only moved to Kingslandshire after the forcible partition of Ashenwyrm (his wife's birthplace). They moved in with his wife's father who lives with them.
He is a loyalist who loves the king wholeheartedly.

Will has a strong sense of hounour and tradition. He is exceedingly hospitable and would rather go hungry himself than deny food to a guest.

Will sometimes has strong hunches that turn out to be correct, and others know this and listen when he has an idea. He believes everyone is where they are for a reason and believes it is important to be devout. He believes everyone should mind their own business. He prays to Tyr, and his alignment is lawful neutral.

ROLEPLAYING:

  • Speaks with a Southloridian accent (based on Dutch)
