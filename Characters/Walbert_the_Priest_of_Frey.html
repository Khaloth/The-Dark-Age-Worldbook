Walbert the Priest of Frey

Created Wednesday 14 September 2022

Born 1324; died 1372
A priest of Frey (Chauntea). He adopted an orphan during the first year of Godswar as his son, Rodwin Wolbertson. During this time, he adopted about a dozen other children all of which but one became farmhands after the war - the only one being Rodwin who was to become his fosterfather's successor.

Walbert was killed during a goblin raid on his underground shrine (see quest No Rest for the Wicked). He was the last to correspond with Mindzuk Ashburn prior to his diappearance. See his letter to Almarr von Ehernbruck below:

Moſt eſteemed Sage Almarr von Ehrenbruck

I pray þis Letter finds þee in good health: Þou doſt not know me/ but I haue heard of þee through a mutual acquaintance: Worry þyſelf not/ þe whereabouts of þy dwelling-place remain ſafe with me/ and I haue every intention to take þem with me to þe Grave

I am Freyprieſt Walbert of Cryſtalþorp/ and I am writing to þee as part of my Enquiry into þe Diſappearance of my good friend Sage Mindzuk Rauenſon Aſhburn/ þy former Diſciple/ who ſeems to haue vaniſhed off þe face of þe Earth about a Year ago: I am anxious for þe good Man/ for þeſe are Euil times/ and in ſuch Times/ good men ſuffer moſt

I haue no Reaſon to ſuſpect that he is no longer aliue/ but I likewiſe haue no proof to the contrary/ his diſappearance has been ſudden and complete

I do realiſe þe Dire and Perilous Poſition impoſed upon þe Mages during þe War and þerefore I ſhall be fully ſatiſfied with a brief aſſurance of his good Health and no furþer knowledge of my friend imparted

Þou art far from firſt I haue contacted regarding þis matter/ and þus my Hopes are not high: Shouldſt thou be ignorant of his Fate/ I do pray his being þy Student ſhall arouſe Worry and Intereſt as it hath in me: I þerefore venture to haue faith þou mightſt Wiſh to partake in þis Enquiry

It is þerefore right and proper þat I divulge what my Efforts haue yielded þus far: My contact with þe Man abruptly ceaſed during Coldplough of laſt Year: Long prior to þis/ Minduks behaviour was becoming increaſingly erratic and confuſed/ by Firſtharueſt he had become madly obſeſſed with ſomeþing he called 'Þe Elderlords' and was certain 'they' were after him þat his Death was imminent and þat he had to 'protect himſelf' by ſome means he neuer quite explained to me//

In þe laſt few letters I haue received from him/ he had become outright mad/ and went on in long paſſages about ſome man he dubbed 'þe Damned Helldorian' who had 'tranſcended mortal fleſh in ſearch of þe þousand-eyed Throne in þe Lands þe Serpent envenometh not': At þe ſame time he flooded me with Endleß queſtions from þe Holy Elderwrit/ eſpecially þoſe pertaining to Worldsend orcheſtrated by þe Serpent Out of Time

In þe laſt letter I haue receiued from him/ his Madness was unbounded: He told me þe 'Witch of Seuentown' viſited him in a Dream/ and forewarned him þey would come for him at Dawn: He beſought me I forget about þe Matter and warn my Gods þat þey ſhould be next: for 'Men Shall be as Gods and Gods ſhall Be as Men' and 'Death would ſurely Die'

I am at a loß and haue not heard from him ſince: I haue ſince þen receiued a Reſponſe from þe Wondergarden Mages þat Mindzuk left þe Place in great Hurry with a Man named Hellig of whom þey knew little: He left behind a Will in which he made his former/ raþer wayward/ Student Whuum/ a ravenman/ his ſole Heir both in Riches and Honour

I haue ſince þen brought þe Letters to þe local Ogmarſhrine in an attempt to find deeper meaning in þem: but found little ſucceß

I pray/ ſend þy reply to þe Freyſhrine-upon-þe-Lake in Cryſtalþorp: I ſhall eagerly await it

Gods watch ouer þee and protect þy Houſe

Freyprieſt Walbert of Cryſtalþorp
