Example Use

  • Netmortxhosh! I wish you two had died.
  • Netdegghat. I wish they were going to give [it]
  • Niklebxhat mannsixh. The two men will not live.
  • Perd velgeporhorgog kerkgher straghag. Netterst goghug, zetpomet vzharghag. Before the great-terrible ones we fearfully tremble. May he punish others and forget you always.
